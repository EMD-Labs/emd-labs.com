Initiatives

Trash for Trade

  • Trash for Trade is a Plastic Upcycling Social Enterprise.It is a social innovation idea developed from an open-source community, ‘Precious Plastic’ with the support of DreamSpace Academy. We have taken upon this initiative using the upcycling machinery our team has built. Trash for Trade community provides innovative, economic, efficient, reliable, eco-friendly upcycling technology to Sri Lanka along with spreading awareness about its environmental and economic benefits.
